Cyoa Plan

Context

We had to make an adventure plan where there are many choices to choose. The title of this is called A School Where If You Get a Question Wrong, you die.

Content

With our partner, we had to make an adventure plan using the IDE and Github.com. Our genre was about mystery and we made this timeline about you going into a school and you have to answer questions to progress. However if you get one wrong, you die.

Reflection

Some challenges were trying to link the different outcomes to the github. It was so messy at first but we got it organized. Another challenge was trying to think of a good plot. We didn't want it to be too weird, so we just went with something basic when the person chooses that outcome. A takeaway is to communicate with your partner as it allows you to finish your work way faster. If we had more time, I'd probably add more images.
