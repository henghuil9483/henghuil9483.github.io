Movie Webpage Project

Context

We had to make a movie and we had to work with a partner. The movie title is called The Lost.

Content

We had to create a webpage using what we learned so far using HTML and CSS. The genre was about mystery and we made this story about people going missing this past serveral days due to a fog. Thus there will be a detective trying to find out where the missing people has gone to.

Reflection

Some challenges were trying to think of a good plot and also the title. We didn't want to make it sound too classic or generic. We ended up just making the story pretty basic about people getting lost. Another challenge was the CSS part. We didn't know what background colors to add and what images to add either. A takeaway is to try and communicate with your partner so the work will be easier.
